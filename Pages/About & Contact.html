The Producer

Harish Cousal

ToneVault is the creative engine and sonic laboratory of Harish Cousal. Designed to push the boundaries of modern electronic music, ToneVault specializes in blending hard-hitting, bass-heavy drops with the emotional depth of cinematic orchestral arrangements.

Every project that comes through the vault is treated with obsessive attention to detail—whether it's a full custom production, mixing and mastering a client's track, or designing premium sample packs for fellow creators. The goal is simple: create music that moves, resonates, and speaks a language beyond words.
