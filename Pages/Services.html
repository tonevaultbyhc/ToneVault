How The Vault Works

1

The Drop-In

We align on your creative vision. You share your reference tracks, demos, and the specific emotional or energetic vibe you want to achieve for the project.

2

The Lab

I dive into the studio, building the core arrangement, heavy sound design, and structure of the track. A rough draft is sent your way to ensure we are locked onto the right frequency.

3

The Polish

We run through targeted revisions. We tweak the mix, adjust the drops, and fine-tune the cinematic transitions until the arrangement is absolutely perfect.

4

The Master

The final stage. You receive the high-fidelity WAV and MP3 files, fully optimized, mastered, and completely ready for global release across all platforms.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I retain the rights to my music?
Yes. For full custom production and mixing services, you retain 100% of your writing and publishing royalties. ToneVault operates on an upfront work-for-hire basis unless a different royalty split is mutually negotiated beforehand.
What genres do you specialize in?
My primary expertise lies in heavy Electronic Dance Music (EDM), bass-driven genres, and hybrid Orchestral Cinematic arrangements. I blend electronic energy with deep, emotional soundscapes.
What is the typical turnaround time?
Turnaround times vary depending on the scope of the project. Mixing and mastering usually take 3-5 days. Full custom production and cinematic scoring projects typically range from 2 to 4 weeks depending on the complexity of the arrangement and the revision process.
